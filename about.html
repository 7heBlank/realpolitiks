About

At the R.P.P.C., our goal is to provide information on international issues and points of attention.

For students at CHHS, such information will be a valuable asset for the AP English 11 test, which

requires students to pull outside evidence and information on current events to write an essay.

Additionally, we believe that having a basic knowledge of events outside the US will allow us to

make better life decisions, especially as many of us start to move from high school and into

college. Soon, you will be the ones to vote for our leaders and shape the world as we know

it, and it is essential that such decisions are well informed.
